About This Activity

City Running Tours - San Diego Choose how far you would like to run, what areas and sight you would like to see and we will customize a run for you. Your running guide will meet you where you are staying to take you on your run.

We will run at your pace, provide water and bring a camera in case you feel like capturing the moment. Once your run is complete your running guide will return you back where you are staying.

More Details

The cost of your City Running Tour is dependent on the distance selected and number of runners registered. It is full price for 1 runner and 10% off each additional runner. The discount will be automatically deducted.

If you are registering for more than one run you will receive $10 off on the 2nd run and $20 off on the 3rd run. Please make sure to select how many runners. For multiple runners the 10% discount per runner will automatically be deducted.
